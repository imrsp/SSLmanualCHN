About This Project

This is a volunteer-led Chinese translation of the SSL Live console help system, maintained by DMT Club (Digital Media Technology Club). Our goal is to make professional audio technology more accessible to Chinese-speaking engineers.

Features

  • Complete Translation — Full Chinese translation of the SSL Live help system, spanning all sections from console introduction to advanced configuration.
  • Side-by-Side Reference — Toggle between Chinese and English at any time for comparison.
  • Full-Text Search — Search across all topics in both languages.
  • Responsive Design — Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile browsers.

Open Source

This project is entirely open source. Contributions, corrections, and suggestions are welcome.
